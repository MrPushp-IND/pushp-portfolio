03 / SQL · Capital Allocation

ITC Ltd FY25 — Segment Profitability & Capital Allocation

A SQL capital-allocation analysis of ITC's ₹81,613 Cr FY25 segment financials — answering the board-level question: which businesses earn their cost of capital, and where should the next rupee go?

Problem
  • Four very different businesses, one capital pool — margin-only views couldn't say which segments deserved investment
  • Strategy directs growth capital toward the lowest-returning segments, quietly diluting group returns
Solution · Insight · Recommendation

Recommendation: reallocate toward returns
Fund the highest-ROCE growth (Agri) first; set return-improvement targets for below-hurdle segments before committing more capital.
